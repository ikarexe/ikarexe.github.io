Yogaratova is a distraction-free calendar for planning your yoga practice. Schedule sessions at set hours, build a personal sadhana routine, and mark practices as complete to keep your habit consistent.

  • Schedule practices at specific hours of the day
  • Personalized sadhana routine
  • Mark completed sessions
  • Track history by day, week and month
  • Simple, distraction-free design
