2D Battle Simulator: Tabs is a sandbox battle simulator. Pick units from multiple faction tabs, place them on a 2D battlefield and watch the fight play out on its own.

  • 100-level campaign
  • Zombie Mode and a free-form Sandbox mode
  • Multiple unit tabs and factions to mix and match
  • Local multiplayer on a single device
  • 100,000+ downloads on Google Play
