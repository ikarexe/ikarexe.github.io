2D Battle Online is a multiplayer browser strategy inspired by Direct Strike from StarCraft II. You don't control units directly: spend resources on units, army upgrades and economy, and your army spawns in waves and fights on its own in the middle of the field. Destroy the enemy team's bases to win.

  • 1v1, 2v2 and 3v3 rooms
  • Auto-battler flow: buy units, watch waves clash — no micromanagement
  • Runs right in the browser, no install
  • Currently in development
