Tickby is a clean, fast personal finance tracker that helps you see where your money goes — without ads, clutter, or sign-up walls. Add an expense in a couple of taps, watch your spending and income come together in a simple Overview, and keep everything private.

Built offline-first: the app works fully without an account or internet. Sign in only if you want to back up and sync across devices.

Why Tickby

  • Fast entry — tap a category, type the amount, done.
  • Offline-first — no account required, your data stays on your device.
  • Private by design — optional end-to-end encryption means only you can read your data.
  • Clean, distraction-free design with light and dark themes.

Track your money

  • Organize spending and income into custom categories with your own icons and colors.
  • Quick-add transactions right from the home screen.
  • Searchable history with filters to find any transaction in seconds.

Understand your spending

  • Overview dashboard with separate Expenses and Income views.
  • Spending and income charts across days, weeks, months, and more.
  • Compare against the previous period and spot your biggest expense or income.
  • Multi-currency support with up-to-date exchange rates.

Sync & backup (optional)

  • Sign in with Google to back up your data and sync across your devices.
  • End-to-end encryption: your data is encrypted on your device before it ever reaches the cloud. You get a 12-word recovery phrase — only you hold the key.
  • Export and import a full JSON backup at any time.

Private by default

  • No ads. No tracking. No selling your data.
  • Use it 100% offline, or turn on encrypted cloud sync — your choice.

Made for everyone

  • Available in many languages, including English, Spanish, German, French, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Arabic, and more.
  • Light and dark themes that follow your system or your preference.

Start tracking your money the simple way. Download Tickby and take control of your finances today.
