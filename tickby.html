Tickby is an offline-first personal finance tracker: add an expense in a couple of taps, see spending and income come together in a simple Overview, and keep everything private — no ads, no account required.

Track your money

  • Custom categories with your own icons and colors.
  • Quick-add transactions from the home screen; searchable, filterable history.
  • Overview dashboard with charts by day, week, month and period-over-period comparison.
  • Multi-currency support with up-to-date exchange rates.

Sync & privacy

  • Fully offline by default — sign in only to back up and sync across devices.
  • Optional end-to-end encryption: data is encrypted on-device, unlocked with a 12-word recovery phrase only you hold.
  • Full JSON export/import, no ads or tracking, light and dark themes, and support for many languages.
